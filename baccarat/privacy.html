Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Baccarat ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

About the App

Baccarat is a simulated gambling game for entertainment purposes only. No real money is wagered, won, or lost. All in-game currency is virtual and has no real-world monetary value. The App is rated 17+ and is not intended for users under the age of 17.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Game data (such as chip balance and game history) is stored only on your device and is not sent to any server.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location information
  • App usage data and interaction with ads

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings.

Consent for Users in the EEA/UK

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we will request your consent before serving personalized ads, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You may choose to receive non-personalized ads instead. You can update your consent preferences at any time by reinstalling the App.

Age Restriction

This App is intended for users aged 17 and older. It contains simulated gambling content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 17.

Data Retention

The App does not store any personal data on external servers. All app data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.
